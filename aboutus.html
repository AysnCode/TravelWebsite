Who are we?

Huan Dao Travel and Tours is one of the leading tour operators in the Philippines providing comprehensive services for groups inside the country. Huan Dao travel was first established on June of 2007. It compromises local tours and usually cater Chinese inbounds. After a decade of good service, it has created a big name in travel and tours industry. Awards inside its home country have been given through the years of its existence.

How does Huan Dao benefits its clients? Huan Dao Travel and Tours have an excellent reputation for personal services, quality and value. Theirs agents travel to the places we sell to check the newest properties along with the older properties that have undergone renovation. For Huan Dao Travel and Tours, this is an important aspect for being an above average. It enables us to suggest a place that fits our client's needs.

Huan Dao Travel and Tours consultants are friendly, professional, and experienced in accommodating both the new and the seasoned travelers. Huan Dao Travel and Tours pride good performance in customer satisfaction. A large percentage of our bookings are from our previous clients.

History

Huan Dao was founded in 2007 by Sheila Velez, a filipino-chinese businesswoman. Knowing that people love to travel around the world, she has thought of an idea of establishing a travel agency. With her family and friend's support, she managed to make her travel agency grow and be known around the Philippines. In its decade of service, she have proved that Huan Dao Travel is a worth it choice to build with.

Huan Dao Travel and Tours mission is to inspire people to travel the world and start in the Philippines. We are dedicated to provide our customers their "Happy Places" in their travel needs.
